How It Works

Quick guide to the planner

  • Three columns for P3, P4, and P5. Click "+ Add Course" to browse and select.
  • Priority sliders rank courses by your interests. Adjust to see different recommendations.
  • Top Picks shows the 20 best courses for you based on your slider settings.
  • Shortlist courses you're considering but haven't committed to yet.
  • Bidding table helps plan your P3 bid allocation with backup courses.
  • Credit constraints update live: 10.5 min, 12.5 max total.
